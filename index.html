About the Backstreet Boys

Backstreet Boys is an American vocal group formed in Orlando, Florida in 1993. The group consists of Nick Carter, Howie Dorough, AJ McLean, and cousins Brian Littrell and Kevin Richardson.

Backstreet Boys have released four albums since 1996. The group rose to superstardom with their debut international album, Backstreet's Back (1996) After a two-year hiatus, they regrouped and released a comeback album Never Gone (2005) In 2006, Richardson left the group to pursue other interests.

The Backstreet Boys have sold over 100 million records worldwide. They are the best-selling boy band of all time, and one of the world's best-known music artists. The group has a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame and plans to play a Las Vegas residency show.

The Backstreet Boys are coming to you Summer 2021! Don't miss out!
